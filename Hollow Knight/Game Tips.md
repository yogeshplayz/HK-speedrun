# Hollow Knight Tips and Tricks

## Combat Tips
- **Pogo Jump**: Use downward strikes on enemies to bounce and gain height. Essential for navigating and combat.
- **Dash Strike**: Combine dashing with a slash for increased damage and knockback.
- **Wall Jumps**: Master wall sliding and wall jumping for mobility. Hold against walls to slide down slowly.
- **Charm Synergies**: Combine charms like Mark of Pride + Longnail for extended reach, or Unbreakable Strength + Heavy Blow for damage.

## Navigation & Exploration
- **Mantis Claw**: Prioritize getting this charm early for wall climbing mobility.
- **Crystal Heart**: Enables dashing through certain obstacles and environmental hazards.
- **Isma's Tear**: Required to swim in acid pools safely. Essential for many areas.
- **Kingsoul**: Carries benchwarp functionality - practice efficient backtracking routes.

## Bosses & Enemies
- **Dancing with Bosses**: Learn attack patterns and practice spacing. Most bosses have safe windows after attacks.
- **Invincibility Frames**: Use these frames strategically when getting hit to reduce damage.
- **Protective Charms**: Deep Focus heals more but is slower. Equip based on boss patterns.
- **Spell Efficiency**: Practice spell casting combos (Vengeful Spirit + Shade Soul).

## Speed Running Specifics
- **Sequence Breaks**: Know which areas can be accessed out of order with specific charms or techniques.
- **Bench Spawning**: Remember where benches are located for quick travel between areas.
- **Movement Optimization**: Master the Pale Ore route to gain Unbreakable Charm upgrades faster.
- **Mask/Vessel Shards**: Prioritize collecting shards needed for health/soul increases.

## Charm Recommendations
- **Mobility**: Mantis Claw, Crystal Heart, Dash Master
- **Offense**: Mark of Pride, Unbreakable Strength, Sharpening Stone
- **Defense**: Unbreakable Heart, Quick Slash, Thorns of Agony
- **Utility**: Isma's Tear, King's Brand (for locked doors)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid
- Don't overextend - positioning beats aggression
- Don't waste soul on unnecessary healing - heal only when necessary
- Avoid getting cornered by enemies; maintain escape routes
- Don't neglect charm combinations - single charms are often underwhelming

## Grind Spots
- **Pale Ore**: Found in specific boss encounters and unique locations
- **Geo Farming**: Deepnest offers good Geo yields for charm upgrades
- **Vessel Shards**: Complete dream boss encounters for optimal efficiency

## Pro Tips
- Practice parrying attacks with perfectly timed strikes
- Use the terrain to your advantage in combat situations
- Memorize boss attack patterns before attempting on harder difficulties
- Keep track of charm slots - strategic removal and equipping is key
