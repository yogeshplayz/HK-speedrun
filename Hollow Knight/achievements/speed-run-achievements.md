# Speed Run Achievements Guide

## Under 5 Hours
Complete the game in under 5 hours.

### Route Overview
- Skip optional bosses when possible
- Collect essential upgrades only
- Use the most efficient path through zones
- Minimize backtracking

### Key Checkpoints
- Tutorial completion: ~5 minutes
- First boss defeat: ~20 minutes
- Kingdom's Edge entrance: ~1.5 hours
- Final boss encounter: ~4 hours
- Victory: Under 5 hours

---

## Under 3 Hours
Expert speedrun - complete in under 3 hours.

### Requirements
- Advanced movement tech knowledge
- Optimal charm usage
- Perfect routing
- Minimal exploration

### Advanced Techniques
- Wall jumping precision
- Double jumping with Mothwing Cloak
- Charm switching mid-combat
- Sequence breaking when available

---

## Any% Route
Fastest completion using any means necessary.

### Route Essentials
1. Skip all optional upgrades if possible
2. Use sequence breaks to access end-game areas
3. Minimize boss encounters
4. Head straight to final objective

### Time Breakdown
- Early game: 15 minutes
- Mid game: 45 minutes
- Late game: 30 minutes
- Final boss: 10 minutes
- **Total: ~100 minutes**

---

Complete everything as fast as possible.

### Requires
- All masks and vessels
- All charm notches

- All boss defeats

### Estimated Time
- Typically 6-8 hours for experienced runners
