# Combat Achievements Guide

## Boss Rush
Defeat all boss encounters in succession without leaving the arena.

### Requirements
- All healing items used wisely
- Knowledge of boss attack patterns
- Charm optimization

### Strategy
1. Practice individual bosses first
2. Learn dodge patterns for each encounter
3. Use offensive charms like Strength or Longnail
4. Manage charm notches carefully

---

## Pantheon of Hallownest
Complete the ultimate challenge featuring all bosses.

### Tips
- Stock up on healing before entering
- Consider charm loadouts for different boss types
- Practice patience - this is an endurance test
- Take breaks between attempts

---

## Trial of the Conqueror
Defeat specific boss combinations with limited resources.

### Recommended Charms
- Unbreakable Strength
- Mark of Pride
- Quick Slash
- Spell Twister

---

## Absolute Radiance
The ultimate challenge - defeat Absolute Radiance without taking damage.

### Requirements
- Complete mastery of dodge mechanics
- Understanding of all attack patterns
- Perfect charm setup
- Hours of practice

### Attack Phases to Know
- Phase 1: Beam attacks
- Phase 2: Melee combos
- Phase 3: Hybrid attacks
- Phase 4: Particle spam
- Phase 5: Final test of skill
