# Exploration Achievements Guide

## Map Completion
Discover 100% of all room layouts.

### Strategy
- Use the Crystal Heart ability to reach high areas
- Dash through narrow passages with Mothwing Cloak
- Unlock all upgrades for mobility
- Refer to interactive maps for missing areas

### Key Regions to Explore
1. Kingdom's Edge
2. The Abyss
3. Ancient Basin
4. Royal Waterways
5. Kingdom's Edge

---

## All Upgrades
Collect all vessel fragments, mask shards, and charm notches.

### Vessel Fragments (8 total)
- 1 from defeating Uumuu
- 1 from defeating Royal Waterways boss
- Multiple hidden throughout the kingdom

### Mask Shards (16 total)
- Increase maximum health
- Found in challenging areas and boss rewards

### Charm Notches (4 total)
- Sell to Salubra or find in specific locations
- Critical for charm combinations

---

## Pantheon of Hallownest Completion
Complete all pantheon levels.

### Pantheon Levels
- Pantheon of Master: 10 bosses
- Pantheon of Artist: 15 bosses
- Pantheon of Sage: 20 bosses
- Pantheon of Knight: All bosses
- Pantheon of Hallownest: Absolute Radiance

---

## Hidden Charm Discovery
Find all 40+ charms in the game.

### Charm Types
- Offensive: Increases damage output
- Defensive: Provides protection
- Movement: Enhances mobility
- Utility: Special effects

### Tips for Finding Rare Charms
- Look behind destructible walls
- Complete optional questlines
- Trade with NPC characters
- Defeat hidden mini-bosses
