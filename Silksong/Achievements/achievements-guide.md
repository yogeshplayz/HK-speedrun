## Story Achievements

### The Beginning
**Description:** Start a new game
**How to Unlock:** Simply create a new game file
**Difficulty:** ⭐ (Trivial)

### Into the Depths
**Description:** Enter a new area
**How to Unlock:** Travel to any new area beyond the starting region
**Difficulty:** ⭐ (Trivial)

### Silksong Complete
**Description:** Reach the end credits
**How to Unlock:** Complete the main story by defeating the final boss
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Hard)
**Tips:** Follow the main speedrun route or enjoy the game at your own pace

### Steel Soul Victory
**Description:** Complete the game in Steel Soul mode
**How to Unlock:** Finish the game in Steel Soul difficulty (permanent death mode)
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Extreme)
**Tips:** Refer to the Steel Soul speedrun guide for optimal route planning

## Combat Achievements

### Master of Needles
**Description:** Fully upgrade the Needle
**How to Unlock:** Collect all Needle upgrade materials and complete all upgrades
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium-Hard)
**Tips:** 
- Needle upgrades are found throughout the game
- Follow the speedrun routes for efficient collection
- Maximum needle level is 5

### Boss Slayer
**Description:** Defeat 20 unique bosses
**How to Unlock:** Hunt and defeat 20 different boss encounters
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Hard)
**Tips:**
- Some bosses have multiple encounters
- Dream bosses in Act 3 are good for farming
- Use Magma Bell for fire-type bosses

### Unravelled Master
**Description:** Defeat The Unravelled on steel soul
**How to Unlock:** Beat the final boss in Steel Soul mode
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Extreme)
**Tips:**
- This is the hardest boss in the game
- Ensure you have max masks, tools, and upgrades
- Learn attack patterns before attempting

### Dream Warrior
**Description:** Defeat 10 dream bosses
**How to Unlock:** Complete 10 dream boss encounters
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium-Hard)
**Tips:**
- Dream bosses are unlocked through wishes
- They become easier in Act 3 (void forms)
- Can be done multiple times for practice

## Exploration Achievements

### Map Collector
**Description:** Discover 50% of the map
**How to Unlock:** Explore and unlock map areas covering half the game world
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐ (Easy-Medium)
**Tips:**
- Use Shakra's maps to reveal areas
- Follow main story progression naturally

### Full Atlas
**Description:** Discover 100% of the map
**How to Unlock:** Unlock every area in the game world
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium-Hard)
**Tips:**
- Requires thorough exploration
- Some areas require specific abilities (Silk Soar, Clawline, etc.)
- Buy all available maps from Shakra

### Hidden Depths
**Description:** Discover a secret room
**How to Unlock:** Find any hidden/secret area in the game
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐ (Easy-Medium)
**Tips:**
- Secret rooms are often behind walls or in unexpected locations
- Look for cracks, suspicious walls, or passages

### Scenic Route
**Description:** Reach every area in the game
**How to Unlock:** Visit every location/area in Silksong
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium-Hard)
**Tips:** Combined with Full Atlas achievement

## Skill-Based Achievements

### Parkour Master
**Description:** Complete a movement challenge without taking damage
**How to Unlock:** Successfully navigate a parkour section flawlessly
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium-Hard)
**Tips:**
- Practice movement abilities first
- Learn optimal routes through challenging areas
- Use invulnerability frames wisely

### Perfect Parry
**Description:** Parry 5 consecutive attacks
**How to Unlock:** Successfully block/parry 5 enemy attacks in a row
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium-Hard)
**Tips:**
- Time your parries carefully
- Practice in low-stakes combat first
- Use against basic enemies to build confidence

### Swift Striker
**Description:** Defeat a boss in under 2 minutes
**How to Unlock:** Beat any boss with a time limit of 2 minutes
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Hard)
**Tips:**
- Choose easier bosses for your first attempts
- Learn boss patterns to minimize damage taken
- Use optimal damage tools/upgrades

### Speedrunner
**Description:** Complete the game in under 20 hours
**How to Unlock:** Finish the main story within 20 hours of playtime
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Hard)
**Tips:**
- Follow speedrun routes provided in this repository
- Minimize backtracking
- Skip optional content

## Collection Achievements

### Mask Collector
**Description:** Collect all mask shards
**How to Unlock:** Gather all mask shard pieces in the game
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium-Hard)
**Tips:**
- 10 total masks (40 shards) available
- Some are in combat challenges
- Others are hidden or behind ability gates
- Use the speedrun guides for efficient routes

### Tool Master
**Description:** Collect all tools
**How to Unlock:** Obtain every tool in the game
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium-Hard)
**Tips:**
- Tools are located throughout the world
- Some require specific abilities to access
- Purchase missing tools from merchants

### Memory Keeper
**Description:** Collect all memory lockets
**How to Unlock:** Find and collect every memory locket
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium-Hard)
**Tips:**
- Memory lockets complete the Hunter's Crest
- 10 total memory lockets
- Follow speedrun routes for locations

### Eva Complete
**Description:** Fill all slots in Eva's vesticrest
**How to Unlock:** Maximize Eva's inventory to 32 slots
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Hard)
**Tips:**
- Requires extensive grinding and completion
- Memory lockets are needed
- Long-term achievement requiring many game hours

## Miscellaneous Achievements

### Wish Master
**Description:** Complete all wishes
**How to Unlock:** Finish every wish in the game
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Hard)
**Tips:**
- 30+ wishes available
- Some are story-related
- Others require specific conditions
- Refer to wish guides for detailed instructions

### Flea Festival Champion
**Description:** Catch 100 fleas
**How to Unlock:** Capture 100 fleas throughout the game
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium-Hard)
**Tips:**
- Fleas appear in specific locations
- Can be grinded in Fleatopia
- Time-consuming but straightforward

### Journal Virtuoso
**Description:** Complete the hunter's journal (all entries)
**How to Unlock:** Document every enemy and boss in the game
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐ (Medium-Hard)
**Tips:**
- Requires defeating/encountering all enemies
- Some journal entries only appear in specific modes
- Track progress regularly

### Everbloom
**Description:** Collect the Everbloom charm
**How to Unlock:** Complete specific requirements to obtain this legendary item
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Hard)
**Tips:**
- Requires high completion percentage
- Likely endgame content
- Check advanced guides for specific requirements

### No Damage Run
**Description:** Complete a story section without taking damage
**How to Unlock:** Progress through a designated area without being hit
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Hard)
**Tips:**
- Practice defensive mechanics
- Learn enemy patterns thoroughly
- Consider disabling certain attacks with equipment

### Platinum Knight
**Description:** Unlock all other achievements
**How to Unlock:** Complete every achievement in the game
**Difficulty:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Extreme)
**Tips:**
- This is the ultimate achievement
- Requires dedication and skill
- Estimate 100-150+ hours for completion
- Use all guides in this repository for reference

## General Tips for Achievement Hunting

1. **Play Naturally First** - Enjoy the game without focusing on achievements initially
2. **Use Speedrun Routes** - Refer to the speedrun guides for optimal paths when needed
3. **Plan Your Route** - Decide which achievements to prioritize to minimize backtracking
4. **Practice Combat** - Skill-based achievements require practice and pattern recognition
5. **Be Patient** - Some achievements require grinding; take breaks to avoid burnout
6. **Document Progress** - Track which achievements you've completed
7. **Watch Guides** - YouTube tutorials can help with difficult boss fights or secret locations
